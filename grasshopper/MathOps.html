Math Operators

In coding, math operators are used to perform arithmetic operations on numbers.

Plus Operator

The + operator adds two values together. This operator can also be used to combine strings.

Minus Operator

The - operator subtracts the number on the right side of the operator from the number on the left side.

Multiplication Operator

The * operator multiplies the number on the left side of the operator by the number on the right side.

Division Operator

The / operator divides the number on the left side of the operator by the number on the right side.

Remainder Operator

The % operator, also called the modulus, returns the remainder from dividing the number on the left of the operator by the number on the right.
